Method: axis-aligned bounding-box (AABB) grid packing. Counts the maximum identical copies of the part's bounding box that tile inside the container, trying all 6 axis orientations and picking the best. Real packing of nestable parts (e.g. a U-channel that slots inside another) can exceed this count; mesh-level nesting requires a different algorithm.
